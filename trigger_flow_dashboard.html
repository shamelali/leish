Why the redirect bug disappears

The original gap was not at sign-up itself; it was the missing public.profiles record and artist role at the moment the dashboard layout began validating state. By adding a Postgres trigger on auth.users INSERT, the system now provisions the required profile automatically before the layout reaches its access checks.

This makes the role-based route stable because the layout sees Auth: OK, Profile: OK, and Role: OK as part of one continuous registration outcome rather than as separate, timing-sensitive operations.

What still happens after the artist redirect

The provider record is still absent immediately after registration, so the layout is expected to identify a provider gap. That is not an error in this flow; it becomes the signal to send the user to /artist/onboarding.

In other words, the trigger solves the authentication-to-profile handoff, and the onboarding redirect continues the product journey by collecting the remaining provider-specific information.
