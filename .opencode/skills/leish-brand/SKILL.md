## Brand tokens
- Background: #f7f4ef (warm parchment)
- Accent: #c4891a (gold)
- Topbar: #1a1410 (dark ink)
- Fonts: Playfair Display (headings), DM Mono (body)

## Component conventions
- Border radius: 2–4px (not rounded)
- Buttons: uppercase, 12px, letter-spacing 0.06em
- Priority badges: high/med/low with opacity backgrounds
